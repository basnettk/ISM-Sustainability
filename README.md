# ISM Sustainability Project

Welcome to the **ISM Sustainability Project**! This website is dedicated to showcasing the environmental initiatives and sustainable practices at International School Manila (ISM). Our goal is to educate the community, promote eco-conscious behaviors, and highlight ongoing efforts such as energy conservation, waste reduction, and the "Bring Your Own Container" (BYOC) program. The platform also features an interactive AI chatbot to help answer any questions related to sustainability at ISM.
