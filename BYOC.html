Bring Your Own Container

What is Bring Your Own Container (BYOC)?

BYOC stands for Bring Your Own Container. At ISM's Kantina, single-use containers are not provided, so students are encouraged to bring reusable lunch boxes or water bottles. This helps reduce plastic waste and promotes more sustainable habits on campus. Since 14% of all litter in the world comes from single-use beverage containers, reducing their use can make a significant difference in lowering overall waste.

Why do we BYOC?

BYOC is encouraged to reduce the use of single-use plastics and lower overall waste on campus. By bringing reusable containers, students help cut down the amount of litter produced daily in the Kantina. It also promotes more sustainable habits by making reuse a normal part of school life. Over time, this reduces environmental impact and supports cleaner surroundings. It’s a simple action that helps build a more responsible and eco-friendly community.

ISM contributes SDG 11 through initiatives like BYOC, which reduce waste and help make the campus more sustainable.
