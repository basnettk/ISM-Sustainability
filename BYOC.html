Bring Your Own Container

BYOC stands for Bring Your Own Container. In the Kantina ISM do not serve single use containers and support students who bring their own lunch box or water bottle. 14% of all litter is from single use beverage containers.

SDG 12 is about responsible consumption and reducing waste. BYOC supports this by reducing single-use plastics through reuse of containers. This helps lower waste and promotes more sustainable habits.
