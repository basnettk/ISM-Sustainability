Compost

Composting turns unwanted items such as food scraps, manure, yard trimmings or wood into suitable soil fertilizer. Compost is specifically beneficial since it reduces waste that would otherwise be dumped and disposed of in landfills.

  1. Reduction in soil erosion
  2. Improved plant growth
  3. Increased plant nutrition
  4. Higher crop yield
  5. Aiding in ecosystem restoration
  6. Increased Biodiversity
