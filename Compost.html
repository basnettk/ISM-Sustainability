About Us BYOC Electricity Compost Summary

Composting turns unwanted items such as food scraps, manure, yard trimmings or wood into suitable soil fertilizer. Compost is specifically benificial since it reduces waste that would otherwise be dumped and disposed of in landfills. Using compost in soil allows for better soil fertility and furthermore provides a list of benefits such as:

  1. Reduction in soil erosion
  2. Improved plant growth
  3. Increased plant nutrition
  4. Higher crop yield
  5. Aiding in ecosystem restoration
  6. Increased Biodiversity
