Our Commitment to Sustainability

Who We Are

International School Manila (ISM) is a vibrant, diverse community deeply committed to environmentality and sustainability. As a leading "green school" in the region, we recognize our responsibility to the planet and to future generations. We are students, educators, and staff united by a common goal, to integrate sustainable practices into every part of our school, from the classroom to our daily lives and community projects.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster an eco-conscious mindset within our community, helping individuals to make informed, responsible choices that positively impact the environment. We wnat to create a culture of sustainability where environmental awareness is part of our curriculum and extracurricular activities. We aim to help out students with the knowledge, skills, and passion necessary to become global citizens and leaders in environmentality.

Taking Action

At ISM, sustainability is not just a concept; it is an active practice. We engage in a wide array of initiatives designed to minimize our ecological footprint. Through waste reduction programs, energy conservation efforts, and the promotion of sustainable practices like our "Bring Your Own Container" (BYOC) initiative, we are constantly working to improve our environmental performance. We believe that through education, action, and continuous improvement, ISM continues to pave the way for a more sustainable and greener future for all.
