Our Commitment to Sustainability

International School Manila (ISM) is deeply committed to environmental sustainability. As a leading "green school," we strive to integrate sustainability into every aspect of campus life, from our classroom curriculums to our daily operations.

We believe that fostering an eco-conscious mindset in our students is vital for the future. Through various initiatives in waste reduction, energy efficiency, and community programs, ISM continues to pave the way for a more sustainable and greener future for all.
