What is SDG's?

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), are a collection of 17 interlinked objectives adopted by the United Nations in 2015 to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity for all by 2030. They act as a shared blueprint for peace and sustainable development.

How does it relate to our website?

In summary, Composting, electricity use, and BYOC (Bring Your Own Container) all connect strongly to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by promoting sustainability and responsible consumption. Composting supports SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) and SDG 13 (Climate Action) by reducing food waste and lowering methane emissions from landfills. It also improves soil health, contributing to more sustainable agriculture.
